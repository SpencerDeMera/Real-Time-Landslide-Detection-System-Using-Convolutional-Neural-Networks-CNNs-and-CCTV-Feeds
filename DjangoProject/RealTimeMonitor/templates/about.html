About LandslideLens


Landslides pose significant risks to infrastructure and human safety, particularly in regions like California, where they are prevalent. Current detection methods rely heavily on manual analysis of satellite imagery, CCTV feeds and reports from authorities and the public. This manual approach is time-consuming and prone to delays, which can hinder timely response and mitigation efforts.


To address these challenges, LandslideLens offers a web-based alert system that leverages Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to detect landslides in real-time from live CCTV feeds and do so automatically. By automating the detection process, the system aims to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of landslide monitoring, providing timely alerts and reducing the dependency on human intervention.
